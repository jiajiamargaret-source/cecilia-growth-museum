Overview

Rethinking waste through imagination.

Future of Trash Conundrum is the opening chapter of Cecilia’s Future Worlds Series. It explores how waste can be reimagined as a resource through creativity, sustainability, and future thinking.

Instead of asking where trash should go, the project asks what trash could become.
